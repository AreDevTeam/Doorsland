Description:

Level 1 (Called The Hub) is a seemingly infinite hallway characterized by a constant 15-degree tilt. The walls are made of white acoustic panels and the floor is covered in old wooden parquet. The most prominent feature of this level is the row of white doors. Doors numbered "2" and "3" are the most common. A low, constant buzzing sound can be heard at all times, coming from the ceiling lights.

Entrances:

  • Finding a specific door in the real world that feels "out of place."
  • No-clipping through a white wall in another stable area.
  • Reaching the specific point known as 1.79E+308.

Exits:

  • Entering a door that has no number may lead to the next level.
  • Walking in the opposite direction of the tilt for an extended period might return you to where you started.
