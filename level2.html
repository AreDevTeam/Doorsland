The Dark Hallway

Hi, I'm C. I'm documenting my descent into this madness. I thought the numbered doors were a way out, but Door 2 led me here. It's pitch black, and my flashlight is the only thing keeping me sane. There's something in the dark with me—I can hear its joints cracking as it moves. I managed to snap a photo of it (File 1000873095_2.png). Stay away from the shadows.

Description:

Level 2 is a drastic shift from the previous area. The lights are non-functional, leaving the environment in near-total darkness. The hallway feels narrower, and the "acoustic panels" from Level 1 appear decayed and stained. A heavy, metallic scent lingers in the air. The main threat here is a tall, distorted entity with elongated limbs that roams the corridor. It is extremely fast and aggressive when it detects light or movement.

Entrances:

  • Entering through the door marked "2" in Level 1.
  • Experiencing a sudden "blackout" while wandering through unstable corridors.
  • Following the sound of scratching behind a white door.

Exits:

  • Finding a door with a glowing red exit sign at the very end of the hall.
  • No-clipping through a section of the floor where the darkness feels "liquid."
